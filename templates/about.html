Welcome to the Liver Disease Predictor!

This tool estimates the likelihood of liver disease based on various health factors with an accuracy of 91%.

Knowing whether you have liver disease is crucial because early detection can significantly improve treatment outcomes and prevent serious complications.

Liver disease can lead to severe health issues, including cirrhosis, liver cancer, and liver failure.

By using this predictor, you can gain insights into your liver health and take proactive steps to seek professional medical advice if necessary.

Please note that this tool is intended for informational purposes only and should not be used as a definitive decision-maker or substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and treatment.
